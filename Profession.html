Roles and professions

Basic Professions are professions that are very common in most planes, they include blacksmiths, weavers, tailors, cooks, innkeepers, barmaids, really any job that is not magic related and is found in nearly all realms.

A Heart §inger is a singer by trade that has incorporated magic into their singing, the basest of Heart §ingers are merely storytellers that bring their stories to life in the senses of the audicence and weaving elaborate tales. However, the least common and most powerful of Heart §ingers are much more than frivolous storytellers, they are trained to the point of uncanny ability, being able to read the hearts and intents of those around them and alter them through singing and telling, making them able to change the nature of those around them and the very best can even bring into being unimaginable horrors and magnificent wonders that are rooted in the hearts of villians and heros. This profession is widely looked down upon and parents often tell their children myths that blame a Heart §inger for the creation of the Forgotten.

Talon Forgers use parts of magic creatures and their innate magic to forge mighty weapons, armors and specialized tools. This profession is more of a scientific job rather than a magic profession.

Jewel Weavers are those who extract the essence of a crystal and weave or infuse it into something or someone. In the case of infusing an object it gains magical resonance and properties based upon the spell. For an unexplanined reason if a Jewel Weaver infuses or weaves crystal essence to a living entity then their optical magic capacity is greatly increased and their eyes take on a crystal structure and shine. There are dire consequences for even the slightest mishap in the process, for both the weaver and the subject.

Metal Weavers are very rare, quite like the resource they use. They create trinkets and wonderous items from flowing metal. Their metal creations have interesting properties when used for mundane as well as magical means.

Stone Shamans are the religious leaders of the desert clans. They use taction magic to form rock hollows and fortresses. They manipulate rock and sand to form homes, structures, towers and, for their raids.

A Knight Resonator is A knight of Teslerax, Zeadrodalar, The void dominion or Miammeoxus who has trained to become a mighty warrior in the ways of sound. They have become so integrated into sound that it is a fundamental part of their physical body.
