      Magics

      The Sonorous realm is magic in nature and therefore has countless magical happenings, thus prohibiting me from detailing all of the magical happenings but I will explain the most prominent and explainable of these magics as best I can.

      The most prominent 6 magics are as follows: Modulation, Incantation, Optic, Taction, Reticent and Wild magic. Modulation is used primarily for instant spells. Incantation is used for enchantment and warding. Optic is the most complicated and mystical. Taction is the most common and simple magic; being based on touch. Reticent is the cursed art of silent magic. Wild magic is the categorization of natural magic that takes effect "randomly", usually in nature.

      Modulation is the use of magic through sound, it is the most straightforward; a specific sound will always create the same effect. Modulation has four elements: Intensity, Tempo, Reliability and Flow. Intensity controls the power of the spell and typically makes the spell's effects more fiery. Tempo controls the speed at which the spell is cast and usually makes the spell more airy. Reliability controls the stability of and length of the spell's effects as well as makes the spell more solid. Flow is your ability to merge and manipulate spells and link unrelated sounds to form cohesive spells, it makes spells more watery.

      Incantation is considered by many to be a subcategory of Modulation as it has the same elements. I will list it as seperate because it has different principles.
